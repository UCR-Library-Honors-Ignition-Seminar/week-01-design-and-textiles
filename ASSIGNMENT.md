# Week 1 Assignment: 2D Design & Textile Foundations

**Due:** End of Week 1 | **Late policy:** 20% deduction; accepted through end of Week 2

---

## Project

Complete **both** of the following:

### Part A: Laser-Cut or Cricut Output
- Modify the "Legend of Zelda Shield" SVG in Inkscape (or design your own vector graphic)
- Output it on the laser cutter or Cricut at the Creat'R Lab
- Document with **at least one photo** of the finished piece

### Part B: Hand Warmer
- Construct a rice-filled hand warmer using patterned cloth
- Use at least one hand-sewing stitch practiced in class
- Document with **at least one photo**

---

## Reflection

Write a **~300-word reflection** using the [reflection template](reflection-template.md).

Copy the template into `week-01/reflection.md` and fill in all four sections.
